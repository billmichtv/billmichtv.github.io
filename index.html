Background
More Than a Headline
Fourteen years covering the Delmarva Peninsula — reporting on a range of stories from wild pony swims to wildfire arson sprees, and from breaking morning news to award-winning long-form documentaries. My career began in upstate New York as a bureau reporter, and I have since evolved into roles such as anchoring, executive producing, and leading in one of the region’s top television stations. In my role as Assistant News Director, I led our editorial strategy, shaping the narrative around the region’s most pressing stories. As the morning anchor, I delivered live broadcasts daily to the Delmarva audience while also shooting, writing, and editing behind the scenes—because I believe exceptional journalism goes far beyond the anchor desk. I am a proud graduate of Syracuse University's S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications (Go Orange!), and my passion for uncovering and sharing compelling stories is unwavering.
